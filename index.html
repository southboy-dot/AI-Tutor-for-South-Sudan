About AI Tutor

Purpose

AI Tutor for South Sudan is designed to provide educational support to primary and secondary school students across South Sudan, regardless of their location or resources. Our aim is to make quality education in Mathematics and Sciences accessible to all students through conversational AI technology.

Primary Basic concepts & foundations
Secondary Advanced topics & exam prep

How It Works

The AI Tutor uses advanced natural language processing to understand student questions and provide tailored educational content for Mathematics and Sciences. By asking a series of questions about the student's education level, existing knowledge, and specific challenges, the tutor creates a personalized learning experience.

Features

  • Personalized learning paths based on student needs
  • Dedicated support for Mathematics and Sciences at primary and secondary levels
  • Interactive quizzes and practice problems with step-by-step solutions
  • Visual explanations of scientific and mathematical concepts
  • Curriculum-aligned content following South Sudan educational standards

Our Vision

We believe that education in STEM subjects is crucial for development, and technology can help bridge educational gaps in regions with limited resources. Our vision is to empower students in South Sudan with the mathematical and scientific knowledge they need to build a brighter future for themselves and their communities.
